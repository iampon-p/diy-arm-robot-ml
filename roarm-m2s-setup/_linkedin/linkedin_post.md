RoArm-M2-S robot arm — setup, drivers, and ROS integration (2025)

Getting hardware to work is harder than most tutorials show.

Set up a complete workspace for the RoArm-M2-S: USB drivers (CP210x), ROS workspace with motion planning, custom 3D attachment files in STEP and STL, and a camera pipeline for visual input.

The bigger goal is integrating this into an automation system for my family's metalwork business in Pattani — where the arm would eventually handle physical sorting tasks.

Still early stages, but hardware-software integration is where real engineering constraints appear: motor torque, serial timing, workspace calibration.
